What we can offer you

Beginning in 2015, the idea to set up catering in Rochester, MN emerged. Since then Emily's Catering was born. The idea was to have catering services focused on primarily American favorites that can be provided for events ranging from smaller casual events to more official events. It's a common experience to bond over food, and the desire is to provide quality food to enhance your event.
